Accessibility is easier to sustain when teams treat it as part of the design system rather than a final QA checklist.

This article walks through focus handling, keyboard navigation, contrast checks, and content structure patterns that hold up well across forms, dialogs, and navigation menus.

You do not need a massive audit process to improve accessibility. Small defaults applied consistently usually deliver the biggest gains.
