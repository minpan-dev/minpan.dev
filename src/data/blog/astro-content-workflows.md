Astro works especially well for teams that want a lightweight publishing workflow without adding a heavy CMS too early.

This post covers folder structure, frontmatter validation, content review habits, and a few practical guardrails that keep markdown-based publishing manageable as a site grows.

The main goal is predictability: contributors should know where content lives, what fields are required, and how posts move from draft to publish.
